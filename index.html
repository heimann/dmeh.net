Hello! I'm a computer programmer currently living in Brooklyn, NY.

Welcome to my corner of the internet. I won't make any promises about what you'll find here, but I generally try to make it a place that I would enjoy if I stumbled onto it myself. I also find it helpful to use it to clarify my own thinking, a sort of map for where I am and where I might be going.

I'm currently working on brus.ai. Before that I built Purple, which we sold to theSkimm in 2019.

I like making things that help people get things done with computers. Most recently I've been working on a bookmarking service that worked more like fuzzy search on top of a text conversation rather than a catalogue.

Tenets

Things that I believe, or ways of seeing the world that I find useful. Think of this as a sort of enumeration of statements that I currently hold to be true, the main purpose is for me to put these down on paper as a way for me to osciffy them.
